ColoringBook® Features


Dive into the fairy world of coloring with the Coloring Book For Me app, the perfect tool to relax, pass the time and release your artistic soul. Find joy and relieve stress with color!


Coloring Book For Me gives you:

- Personalized mandalas: bring your unique designs to life in mandala creation mode
- 'Color by photo' feature: experience a new way of coloring - apply photos taken directly or from Camera Roll to uncolored cells on a coloring page and create matchless works of art in a few taps
- 20+ categories of unique coloring pages from talented illustrators - with a collection that is updated frequently, you'll always have something new to color
- Inspiration feed: explore beautiful works of art created by many members of the art community and publish your own to inspire others
- Your own coloring pictures in the app: import line drawings with the camera or from Camera Roll to color them right on your device
- 40+ beautiful presets Solid and Gradient color palettes: choose the one that fits best and fill the image with colors from soft and muted to bold and lively
- Personal palette: combine any color you like to create a piece of art like no other
- Various coloring tools: color simply with the color-tap tool or get a real coloring experience using markers and pencils
- Fun collection of relaxation sounds
- Post editing effects: add vignettes, change image contours, apply textures & filters
- Apple Pencil support: enjoy an easy and intuitive coloring process on the sharp iPad Pro screen.
